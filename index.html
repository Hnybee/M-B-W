More About M-B-W

Singer, Songwriter and Rapper

Mesmerizing the World with Afro-Fusion

M-B-W electrifies audiences with her bold Afro-fusion sound, blending Nigerian rhythms and global beats while celebrating Black womanhood, delivering infectious grooves, empowering lyrics, and inspiring confidence, self-expression, and empowerment worldwide.

Background M-B-W

Kimberly Ubi was born and raised in Nigeria. She began singing at age six, drawing inspiration from both Nigerian musical traditions and global hip-hop/pop culture. Despite pressure from family to focus on education, Ubi continued to pursue music alongside her studies in Canada. Her artistic persona, M-B-W (mad black woman), reflects both her bold voice and her socially conscious outlook to empower and uplift women career. M-B-W gained early recognition in Nigeria’s underground music circuit through spoken word performances and viral freestyles.

Music-career

M-B-W, aka Kimberly Ubi, is redefining the Nigerian music scene with her bold sound and style! Born in Nigeria, she blends traditional vibes with global hip-hop and pop culture. Her persona, M-B-W (Mad Black Woman), empowers women and celebrates African culture. Her music fuses Afro-fusion, pop, rap, and soul, exploring self-expression, femininity, and inner conflict. Influences include Lauryn Hill, Nicki Minaj, Tiwa Savage, and Davido. Debut EP "Athena" (2024) showcased her emotional lyricism and commercial appeal, with tracks like "Lovely" and "Wild". M-B-W shared stages with Brymo at 'LiveWave Concerts' in Wave Beach, proving her live prowess. Latest releases "Mad Party" and Valentine's special "Nasty" are rocking Nigerian clubs. "Mad Party" is a fan favorite, blending Afrobeat with empowering lyrics. M-B-W is working on "Nasty E.P" and a documentary on identity and womanhood. With her bold voice and genre-bending sound, she's an artist to watch, pushing boundaries and celebrating Black womanhood.
*Style & Fashion* M-B-W's cultural and luxury sense of fashion sets her apart. She blends traditional African prints with high-end fashion, creating a unique style that's both empowering and elegant. From bespoke outfits to statement accessories, she's a fashion icon in her own right.
